VYRI YANG

I'm currently a PhD researcher in the Division of Computer Science and Engineering at Louisiana State University. My research focuses on merging the artistic with the scientific to develop impactful, user-centered technologies that redefine the boundaries of human-computer interaction. My work spans across areas such as Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Narrative Visualization, Applied Machine Learning, and Data-driven Interactive Agent, aiming to enhance user experiences and foster deeper engagement through innovative, data-driven designs, and narrative-driven technologies.
