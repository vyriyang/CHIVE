Hello, I am Nushrat Jahan Ria

A Ph.D. student, researcher, and graduate assistant in the Division of Computer Science and Engineering at Louisiana State University (LSU). My research focuses on designing and developing decision-support systems to tackle complex socio-technical problems and enhance data-driven decision-making, including Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Applied Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Information Visualization. I have published multiple papers in IEEE Xplorer, Springer, and Scopus-indexed conferences and journals, contributing to machine learning, NLP, and computer vision research. Driven by innovation and inclusivity, my mission is to advance HCI, applied machine learning, and computer vision while inspiring the next generation of programmers and researchers.
